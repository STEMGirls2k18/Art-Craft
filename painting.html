Painting

Painting is much more than a simple activity. It is a way for different types of children to express themselves in their own special way. Painting also helps children use their senses, express emotion, explore process and outcomes, explore color, and create aesthetically pleasing works and experiences

Color used in painting:

  • Water colour

  • Oil pastels

  • Crayons
